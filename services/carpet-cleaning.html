FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does carpet cleaning take?

Most rooms take 20–30 minutes. A typical 3-bedroom home takes about 1.5–2 hours including setup and walkthrough.

How long until my carpets are dry?

Carpets are typically dry within 4–6 hours. We recommend good ventilation and leaving fans on to speed drying.

Do you move furniture?

We move light furniture like dining chairs and small tables at no extra charge. We ask that large or fragile items be moved beforehand.

Is steam cleaning safe for all carpet types?

Yes. We adjust our equipment and solutions based on your carpet fiber type. We handle everything from standard nylon to delicate wool and Berber.
