FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free pickup and delivery?

Yes! We pick up your rugs from your home and deliver them back clean, typically within 5–7 days. No extra charge for pickup and delivery.

Can you clean delicate or antique rugs?

Absolutely. We assess every rug individually and choose the safest cleaning method. Delicate rugs receive low-moisture or dry cleaning treatments.

How long does area rug cleaning take?

We typically return your rugs within 5–7 business days. Rush service may be available upon request.

What's the difference between steam and dry cleaning for rugs?

Steam cleaning uses hot-water extraction for a deep clean — ideal for most rugs. Dry cleaning uses minimal moisture and is better for delicate fibers like silk or antique wool.
