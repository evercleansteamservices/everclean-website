A Cleaner Mattress for a Healthier Sleep

Your mattress accumulates sweat, dead skin cells, dust mites, and allergens over time — even with regular sheet changes. In South Florida's warm, humid climate, mattresses are especially prone to moisture buildup and allergen accumulation. Our mattress cleaning service in Coconut Creek uses professional steam sanitization to deep clean your mattress and create a healthier sleep environment.

  • Quick-dry process — sleep on your mattress the same night

Why Clean Your Mattress?

The average mattress contains millions of dust mites and is one of the biggest allergen sources in your home. If you or your family suffer from allergies, asthma, or respiratory issues, regular mattress cleaning can make a noticeable difference. FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does mattress cleaning take?

A standard mattress takes about 30–45 minutes to clean. We recommend allowing 4–6 hours for drying, though many customers sleep on their mattress the same evening.

Can you remove mattress stains?

We can remove or significantly reduce most stains including sweat, urine, blood, and beverage spills. Very old or set-in stains may not be fully removable but are always improved.

How often should I have my mattress cleaned?

We recommend every 6–12 months, especially for allergy sufferers. At minimum, once a year is a good practice for a healthier sleep environment.

Do you clean mattress toppers and pillows too?

Yes! We can clean mattress toppers and some specialty pillows as part of your mattress cleaning appointment.
