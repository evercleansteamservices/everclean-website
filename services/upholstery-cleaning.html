Your sofa and upholstered furniture see daily use — and over time, body oils, pet hair, food spills, and dust accumulate deep in the fabric. Our Process

We begin with a fabric assessment to determine the safest and most effective cleaning method. For most upholstery, we use hot-water extraction with fabric-safe solutions. For delicate materials, we use low-moisture or dry cleaning methods. Every piece is carefully treated and inspected before we leave.

How much does sofa cleaning cost?

Standard sofa cleaning starts at $250. Sectionals and larger pieces vary. We provide a firm quote before any work begins — no surprises.

Can you clean leather furniture?

Yes! We use specialized leather-safe solutions and conditioners that clean without drying out or damaging the material.

How long does upholstery take to dry?

Most upholstery dries within 2–4 hours. Leather pieces are ready to use almost immediately after cleaning.

Absolutely. We can clean outdoor cushion covers and fabric to remove mildew, pollen, and general wear from the Florida elements.
