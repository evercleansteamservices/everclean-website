Specialized Care for Natural Stone

Marble is beautiful but delicate. Acidic spills, improper cleaners, and daily foot traffic can leave marble looking dull, etched, or stained. Our marble cleaning and polishing service in South Florida uses pH-neutral solutions and professional diamond polishing techniques to restore your stone to its original luster without causing further damage.

What We Offer

  • Deep cleaning of marble floors, countertops, and vanities
  • Honing to remove surface scratches and etch marks
  • Diamond polishing to restore high-gloss shine
  • Sealing to protect against future staining
  • Travertine, limestone, and other natural stone care

Why Marble Needs Professional Care

Unlike tile, marble is a calcium-based stone that reacts with acids (vinegar, lemon juice, even some commercial cleaners). Using the wrong product can cause permanent etching. Our team is trained in proper natural stone care techniques and uses only stone-safe products. We provide custom quotes based on the size, condition, and type of stone in your home.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you remove etch marks from marble?

Yes. Mild etch marks can usually be removed through professional honing and polishing. Severe etching may require multiple passes but can still be significantly improved.

Is marble polishing different from marble cleaning?

Yes. Cleaning removes surface dirt and grime. Polishing is a separate process that uses diamond abrasives to restore the stone's natural shine and remove dullness or scratches.

How often should marble be professionally cleaned?

We recommend professional cleaning every 6–12 months for high-traffic areas, and annual cleaning for lower-traffic marble surfaces. Regular sealing extends the time between professional visits.

Do you work with other natural stones besides marble?

Yes. We clean and polish travertine, limestone, granite, and other natural stone surfaces using methods specific to each stone type.
